about my music

The music I compose is predominantly instrumental, often orchestral. Inspired by film scores, I wish to engage the heart, the imagination, and the senses through my compositions.

more

What sort of music do i make?

My musical education draws from ten years of formal piano instrucion, a lifetime of singing and a passion for voice, self-guided guitar instruction, an interest in world music/forms/instruments, and a range of musical tastes (everywhere from metal to baroque to folk to ambient).

In most cases, I compose my songs by playing each part/voice individually on my keyboard, which is connected to my laptop via MIDI connection. Using Logic Pro X, I mix and match different voices and instruments to each of the tracks I've played, adding effects (e.g. reverb), managing levels, recording, and re-recording until I achieve an acceptable sound and quality! All parts are written, improvised, and performed by me on my keyboard (or, in the occassional case that I use my voice, I employ my own vocal cords).
